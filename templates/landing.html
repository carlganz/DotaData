The Project

This site shows the latest games of Dota2 from all over the world. Included in each of the games' details are the players on each team, which heros were used, which items they bought, how many kills they made and statistical analysis of the games and players. Using your Steam account ID you can look up your Dota2 stats or just browse the most recently played games!
